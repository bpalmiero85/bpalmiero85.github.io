Sign Up

Users can register a new account by filling out the registration form with their first name, last name, email, username, and password. All fields are required, and the email must be valid.

Form Validation

Client-side validation ensures that all required fields are filled correctly. If any field is invalid, an error message is displayed to the user.

Account Creation

Upon successful form submission, the application sends the registration data to the backend. If the username or email is already taken, an error message is displayed.

Email Verification

After successful registration, a verification email is sent to the user's email address. The email contains a verification link that the user must click to verify their account.

Verification Process

Users can verify their account by clicking the link in the verification email. This redirects them to a verification page, where the application confirms their account and logs them in.

Login

Once the account is verified, users can log in with their username and password. If the account is not verified, an error message prompts them to check their email for the verification link.

User Info

After logging in, users can view and edit their account information.

Logout

Users can log out from their account, which clears the session and removes the user cookie. This action ensures that the user must log in again to access protected resources.
