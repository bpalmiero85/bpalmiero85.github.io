Admit a New Jellyfish

Users can admit a new jellyfish into the virtual pet system by filling out a form with necessary details such as name, fullness, hydration, and cleanliness. The form includes validation to ensure all required fields are filled correctly before submission. After clicking the submit button, a card generates with a random jellyfish image and user entered stats.

Feed a Jellyfish

Users can feed a jellyfish to increase its fullness and happiness levels. When a jellyfish is fed, its hydration and cleanliness may decrease slightly. This action refreshes the jellyfish list to show the updated state.

Water a Jellyfish

Users can water a jellyfish to increase its hydration and happiness levels. When a jellyfish is watered, its fullness and cleanliness may decrease slightly. This action refreshes the jellyfish list to show the updated state.

Clean a Jellyfish

Users can clean a jellyfish to increase its cleanliness and happiness levels. When a jellyfish is cleaned, its fullness and hydration may decrease slightly. This action refreshes the jellyfish list to show the updated state.

View Jellyfish List

Users can view a list of all jellyfish in the system. This list displays each jellyfish's name, picture, fullness, hydration, cleanliness, and happiness levels. The list is automatically refreshed whenever an action such as feeding, watering, or cleaning is performed.

Adopt a Jellyfish

Users can adopt a jellyfish. This removes the jellyfish from the list.
